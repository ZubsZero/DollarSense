How to use our App:

Step 1

Press "Next" on the Top right of the screen to head over to the budget converter

Step 2

Add your Monthly income in the monthly income input

Step 3

Add the name of the expense you inserting into the name expense input

Step 4

Add the amount you going to spend on the expense you named in step 3

Step 5

Choose The Category that your expense falls under

Step 6

Click the "Add Expense" button to add it to your list of expenses
